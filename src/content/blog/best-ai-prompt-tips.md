Creating the perfect prompt is both an art and a science. Here are our best tips for getting stunning results from any AI image generator.

## 1. Be Specific and Detailed

The more specific your prompt, the better your results. Instead of "a cat," try:

> "A fluffy Persian cat with striking green eyes, sitting on a velvet cushion, soft studio lighting"

## 2. Use Style Modifiers

Guide the AI's artistic direction with style words:

- **Photorealistic**: "hyperrealistic," "8K," "DSLR photography," "award-winning photograph"
- **Artistic**: "oil painting," "watercolor," "digital art," "concept art"
- **Genre**: "cyberpunk," "fantasy," "steampunk," "vaporwave"

## 3. Describe Lighting and Atmosphere

Lighting transforms images. Try adding:

- "golden hour lighting"
- "dramatic side lighting"
- "soft diffused light"
- "neon glow"
- "cinematic lighting"

## 4. Specify Composition

Tell the AI how to frame your image:

- "close-up shot"
- "wide angle"
- "aerial view"
- "portrait orientation"
- "symmetrical composition"

## 5. Use Negative Prompts

Tell the AI what you don't want:

- "blurry, low quality, distorted"
- "extra limbs, bad anatomy"
- "watermark, text, signature"

## 6. Combine Concepts Creatively

The best results often come from unexpected combinations:

- "A steampunk submarine in a Victorian-era laboratory"
- "A cyberpunk samurai fighting a holographic dragon"
- "An oil painting of a futuristic city in Renaissance style"

## 7. Reference Colors and Mood

Set the emotional tone:

- "warm golden tones"
- "cold blue atmosphere"
- "vibrant neon colors"
- "muted earthy palette"

## Practice Makes Perfect

The key to great prompts is experimentation. Try variations, combine different techniques, and learn from your results. Our unlimited AI image generator is perfect for practice.
